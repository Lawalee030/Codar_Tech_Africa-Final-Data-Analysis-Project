## International Sales Performance 2025 – Final Data Analytics Project - Codar Tech Africa Company

## Project Overview

This project analyzes international sales performance using a data-driven approach to evaluate business performance across customers, products, categories, and store locations. The aim is to generate actionable insights that support informed business decision-making.

## Objectives

* Analyze sales performance and revenue trends
* Evaluate customer purchasing behavior
* Assess product and category performance
* Identify high-performing stores and sales regions
* Generate business insights and recommendations

## Tools Used

* SQL (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
* Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboard)
* Microsoft Excel (Data Preparation)

## Key Findings

* Total revenue generated exceeded **$41 million**
* **Computers** were the top-selling product category
* **United States** generated the highest revenue among countries
* Customer purchasing patterns varied across continents
* High-performing and low-performing products were identified for business improvement

## Recommendations

* Prioritize marketing for top-selling products and categories
* Improve low-performing products through bundling strategies
* Strengthen customer engagement and loyalty programs
* Allocate more resources to high-performing stores
* Optimize pricing strategies to improve sales performance
